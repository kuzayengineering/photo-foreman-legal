Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

Kuzay Engineering ("we", "us", "our") operates the Photo Foreman mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it's used, and your choices.

1. Information We Access

  • Camera: to take job site photos within the App. Photos are stored locally on your device and, if you grant photo library permission, saved to a "Job Site Photos" album in your device's photo library.
  • Location (GPS) and Compass: to record latitude/longitude, compass heading, and approximate address (city/state) for each photo, which are stamped onto the photo as a visible watermark.
  • Photo Library: to save watermarked photos to your device's photo library and, if you delete a photo in the App, to remove it from that library.

2. How We Use Information

All information above is used solely to provide the App's core functionality: capturing, watermarking, organizing, and sharing job site photos. We do not use this information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to third parties.

3. Where Your Data Lives

Photo Foreman does not have its own backend server. All photos, location data, and folder/project organization are stored locally on your device. We do not receive, collect, or have access to your photos or location data.

4. Third-Party Services

To determine the city and state for a photo, the App sends the photo's GPS coordinates to the Nominatim reverse-geocoding service operated by the OpenStreetMap Foundation. This is the only information transmitted off your device by the App. That request is subject to OpenStreetMap's own policies: Nominatim Usage Policy and OSM Foundation Privacy Policy. If you take a photo without location services enabled, no such request is made.

If you use the App's Share feature, photos are handed off to your device's native share sheet, and it's your choice which app or service (email, messaging, etc.) you send them to; that transfer is governed by the recipient service's own privacy policy, not ours.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Photos and their metadata remain on your device until you delete them within the App or uninstall the App. Deleting a photo within the App also attempts to remove the corresponding copy from your device's photo library (in supported builds). Uninstalling the App removes its local data but does not remove photos already saved to your device's photo library.

6. Permissions

You can grant or revoke Camera, Location, and Photo Library permissions at any time through your device's settings. Revoking a permission may limit or disable the corresponding feature (for example, without location permission, photos will be watermarked as "Location unavailable").

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" above.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to kuzayengsupport@gmail.com.
